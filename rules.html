Scoring rules of the Game

  • Perfect Guess: If the number chosen by you equals to the correct number.


  • Excellent Guess: If the chosen number is within 5 units distance from the correct number
    Example - If the correct number was 10 and if you chose in the range 5 to 15 (including 5 & 15) then it will be an Excellent Guess.


  • Good Guess: If the chosen number is within 10 units distance from the correct number
    Example - If the correct number was 20 and if you chose in the range 10 to 30 (including 10 & 30) then it will be a Good Guess.


  • Else the it will be a Bad guess !
